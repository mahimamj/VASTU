What is Vastu Shastra?

Vastu Shastra is the ancient Indian science of architecture and design that harmonizes human dwellings with the natural environment. Dating back over 5,000 years, this holistic system integrates elements of architecture, astronomy, and astrology.

By aligning your living and working spaces with the five elements (Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Space), Vastu creates an environment that promotes health, wealth, prosperity, and overall well-being.
